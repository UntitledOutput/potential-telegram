I

Investment

MCPS's growth depends on both students and the spaces that support them.

School Facilities and Infrastructure.

  • Create reporting systems for students and schools to report infrastructure failures.
  • Have students recount firsthand facility issues directly for an audit created entirely by MCPS students.
  • Prioritize schools that fail to provide clean, safe, and accessible learning environments in future Capital Improvement Plans (CIP).

Mental Health.

  • Strictly enforce minimum counselor-to-student ratios.
  • Encourage MCPS students to utilize their school's psychologists.
  • Normalize and push for excused mental health days with consistent guidelines.
