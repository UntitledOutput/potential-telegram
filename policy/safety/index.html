S

Safety & Support

Students can't learn if they can't feel safe, physically and emotionally.

Bullying Prevention.

  • Reform anti-bullying enforcement in MCPS and provide annual public data on bullying incidents and how they were resolved.
  • Standardize an online countywide reporting system to serve as an alternative to the Bullying, Harassment, or Intimidation Reporting Form.
  • Eliminate policies that may show leniency towards bullying.
  • Train staff more thoroughly in de-escalation and practicing restorative approaches to students.

Student Wellness.

  • Have wellness centers implemented in all middle and high schools throughout MCPS.
  • Allow all middle and high school students to use their cellular devices during lunch.
  • Create a stronger transition support for middle schoolers into high school.
  • Provide universal access to academic recovery options, free of stigma.
